Former EMT turned code nerd. Honing my problem-solving skills while coding apps the solve real life problems. I have a passion for planning, organization, learning, and refactoring until the best solution is reached.

Prior to attending the Nashville Software School I'd done some WordPress web design, as well as some ad designs for a non-profit and a couple of restaurants in New Haven, CT and NYC. I knew that I enjoyed delivering a finished product to the client as much as I loved tinkering on my computer. However, I found my lack of coding skills limiting, which lead me to look into local programs after arriving in Nashville. I chose NSS because of it's Non-profit status, well outlined six-month program, and their commitment to making every student a developer via group projects an agile practices.

I am now pursuing a software development career, bringing to the table problem-solving, attention to detail, and high level written and verbal communication from my medical, food service management, and office administrative background.
